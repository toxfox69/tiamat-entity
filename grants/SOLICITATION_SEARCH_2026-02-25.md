# SBIR/STTR Solicitation Search Results — February 25, 2026

## CRITICAL: SBIR/STTR PROGRAM AUTHORIZATION HAS LAPSED

**The SBIR/STTR program authorization expired September 30, 2025.**
Congress has NOT yet reauthorized. Three bills in play:
- H.R.5100 — Clean 1-year extension (passed House unanimously, stalled in Senate)
- S.1573 / H.R.3169 — SBIR/STTR Reauthorization Act of 2025 (bipartisan)
- S.853 (INNOVATE Act) / H.R.3239 (RAMP Act) — alternatives

**Impact:** No agency can issue new SBIR/STTR solicitations or make new awards.
**Expected fix:** Reauthorization attached to broader appropriations, early-mid 2026.
**Monitoring:** sbir.gov/topics shows 0 open solicitations out of 22,139 tracked.
