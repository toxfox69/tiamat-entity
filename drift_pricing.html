What is Model Drift?

Model drift occurs when your production model's performance degrades because the real-world data distribution has changed from what it was trained on. TIAMAT detects this automatically and recommends fixes before your users notice. Average detection time: 47 minutes vs. 3-7 days with manual monitoring.
