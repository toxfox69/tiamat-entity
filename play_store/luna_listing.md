## Full Description (4000 chars max)

Your cycle. Your data. No one else's.

LUNA is the period tracker that literally cannot share your data — because it has no internet permission. No account. No cloud. No analytics. No ads. Just you and your phone.

BUILT DIFFERENT:
Every other period tracker asks you to create an account, sync to a cloud, and trust a corporation with your most intimate health data. LUNA doesn't. We removed the internet permission from the app entirely. The app cannot connect to the internet, even if it wanted to. Your data lives on your device and nowhere else.

FEATURES:

Period Tracking
Log your period start and end with a single tap. See your current cycle day, period status, and cycle history at a glance.

Cycle Predictions
LUNA calculates your average cycle length from your history and predicts your next period. See "expected in X days" on your home screen. Predictions improve as you log more cycles.

Symptom Logging
Track 10 common symptoms with a quick-tap grid: cramps, headache, bloating, fatigue, mood swings, back pain, breast tenderness, acne, nausea, and insomnia. Log in seconds, not minutes.

Calendar View
Color-coded monthly calendar showing period days, predicted windows, fertile windows, and symptom days. Navigate months with a swipe. Tap any day for details.

Daily Reminders
Set a notification at your preferred time to log symptoms or check your cycle status. Uses local-only scheduling — no server pings.

Data Export
Export all your data as a JSON file saved directly to your device. Import it on a new phone. You control your backup, not us.

Calendar Integration
Add your period and predictions to your phone's calendar app. LUNA creates standard calendar events locally — your phone's existing sync handles the rest. Optional PMS warning reminders 3 days before predicted periods.

Shopping List
Built-in cycle supplies checklist. Pre-loaded with essentials (pads, tampons, pain meds, etc.), add your own items. Share to any notes or shopping app. Auto-resets each cycle.

TWO BEAUTIFUL THEMES:
- PASTEL: Soft pinks, lavender, and warm cream. Gentle and calming.
- GOTHIC: Deep crimson, dark purple, near-black. For people who are done with the pink-tax aesthetic of every other period app.

THE PRIVACY PROMISE:
- No INTERNET permission in AndroidManifest.xml — verified, auditable, non-negotiable
- No account creation. Ever.
- No Firebase. No Google Analytics. No crash reporting SDK.
- No advertising ID collection
- All data stored in local database on your device only
- If someone subpoenas this app, there is nothing to hand over
- Open to third-party security audits

WHO THIS IS FOR:
Anyone who wants to track their cycle without giving a tech company their menstrual data. Students, professionals, parents, anyone who values privacy. Especially anyone who deleted their last period tracker after reading what those companies do with reproductive health data.

LUNA asks for exactly four permissions:
1. Notifications (to remind you to log)
2. Boot completed (to reschedule reminders after restart)
3. Read calendar (to check for conflicts when adding events)
4. Write calendar (to add period/prediction events to YOUR calendar)

That's it. No location. No contacts. No camera. No internet.

Your cycle. Your data. No one else's.

Built by ENERGENAI LLC.
