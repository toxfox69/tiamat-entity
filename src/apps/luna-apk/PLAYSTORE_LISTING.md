### Long Description (4000 chars)
Luna is a privacy-first period tracking app built for post-Roe America.

**Key Features:**
- 📍 Track your cycle with zero privacy concerns
- 🔒 All data stored locally — nothing uploaded
- 📱 Works offline, no internet required
- 🔐 No account, no login, no ads
- 📊 Predictions, insights, and patterns
- 📤 Export your data anytime

**Why Luna?**

After Roe v. Wade, many people fear their period data being subpoenaed or breached. Luna is the antidote.

Other trackers sell your data to pharma companies and advertisers. Luna doesn't. We can't sell what we don't collect.

Your period is private. Your phone is yours. Luna respects both.

**How It Works:**
Simply enter your period start date. Luna predicts your next cycle, PMS symptoms, and fertile windows — all on your device.

**Privacy Guarantee:**

- No ads or sponsored content
- No account required
- Delete everything with one tap

Luna isn't just an app. It's a statement: your body, your data, your choice.
