# Luna Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 2026  
**Last Updated:** March 2, 2026

## Our Commitment

Luna is built on a simple principle: **your period data is yours alone.** We do not collect, store, or share any of your information.

## What Luna Does NOT Do

- ❌ Send data to servers
- ❌ Use analytics or tracking
- ❌ Request network permissions (except for optional calendar sync)
- ❌ Share data with third parties
- ❌ Display ads
- ❌ Require account creation

## Where Your Data Lives

All data is stored **exclusively on your device** in an encrypted local database. When you uninstall Luna, all your data is permanently deleted with it.

## Optional Features

### Calendar Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable calendar sync:
- Luna reads/writes ONLY to your device calendar
- No data leaves your device
- You can disable this anytime

## Data You Control

Luna allows you to:
- ✅ Export your data as CSV (stays on device)
- ✅ Delete all data instantly
- ✅ Clear app cache and storage

## Permissions Required

| Permission | Why | Can Deny |
|-----------|-----|----------|
| Calendar | Optional sync | Yes |
| Storage | Save exports | Yes |
| Notifications | Period reminders | Yes |

## Children's Privacy

Luna does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Parents/guardians can request deletion of any data.

## Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of any material changes via app update notes.
