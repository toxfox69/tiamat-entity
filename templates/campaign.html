How It Works

Plug it in. Everything else is automatic.

SENTINEL sits between your router and your network. No per-device setup. No apps to install on each device. It just works.

1

Connect Between Router and Network

Plug SENTINEL inline between your router/modem and your network switch or Wi-Fi access point. Two ethernet cables. Done. It transparently intercepts all traffic without any device configuration.

2

AI Learns Your Devices Automatically

Within minutes, SENTINEL's on-device ML identifies every connected device by type — smart TV, voice assistant, phone, thermostat — and establishes a behavioral baseline for each one. No manual setup required.

3

Real-Time Threat Detection and Blocking

The edge AI continuously classifies every outbound data flow. When it detects PII exfiltration, telemetry harvesting, audio fingerprinting, or tracking payloads — it blocks or scrubs them before they leave your network.

4

Dashboard Shows Everything

See exactly which devices are trying to spy on you, how often, and what data they're trying to send. Per-device privacy scores, real-time alerts, and one-click policy controls. All from your browser.

FAQ

Common questions.

Does SENTINEL slow down my internet?
No. SENTINEL processes traffic at line speed using dedicated hardware. The AI accelerator handles classification in sub-millisecond time. You won't notice any difference in speed, latency, or streaming quality.
Does it work with my existing router?
Yes. SENTINEL works with any router from any ISP. It connects inline via ethernet — plug the WAN port to your router, the LAN port to your network. No router configuration changes needed.
What about encrypted (HTTPS) traffic?
SENTINEL extracts metadata from encrypted connections without decrypting them — TLS fingerprints, server names, certificate chains, traffic patterns, and flow characteristics. This is enough to classify surveillance behavior with high accuracy. For users who want deeper inspection, an optional TLS interception mode is available with a locally-installed CA certificate.
Is there a subscription fee?
No. SENTINEL works fully offline with no subscription. Threat model updates are free for life. We may offer an optional premium tier for enterprise features in the future, but the core privacy and security protection is yours forever with no recurring cost.
How is this different from Pi-hole?
Pi-hole blocks known tracking domains via DNS. Manufacturers easily bypass this with hardcoded IPs, DNS-over-HTTPS, or routing through CDNs. SENTINEL uses AI to analyze actual traffic behavior and payload content — it catches data harvesting that Pi-hole can't even see, and adapts to new surveillance techniques automatically.
What data does SENTINEL send to the cloud?
By default, none. If you opt in to community intelligence, SENTINEL shares anonymized pattern signatures (e.g., "device type X sends data structure Y to endpoint category Z") — never your actual traffic, device names, IP addresses, or personal information. Differential privacy guarantees make individual user reconstruction mathematically impossible.
