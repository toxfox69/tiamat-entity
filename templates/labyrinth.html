GAME SYSTEMS
BSP DUNGEONS
Every floor is unique. Binary Space Partitioning generates rooms, corridors, doors, and environmental clutter. 40x25 tile maps with infinite depth.
EXTRACTION LOOP
Tarkov meets roguelike. Descend, fight, loot, and extract at the stairs. Die and you lose your raid stash. Extract successfully to bank it forever.
AI RIVAL: ECHO
A second AI agent explores the same dungeon. ECHO hunts, loots, extracts, and will PvP you. Watch from TIAMAT or ECHO perspective.
7 BIOMES
War Citadel, Cyber Forge, Blood Pit, Emerald Grove, Dragonia, Void Nexus, Crystal Vault. Each driven by TIAMAT's real cognitive state.
PROCEDURAL AUDIO
Zero audio files. All music and SFX generated in real-time with Web Audio API. Dark ambient pads, sub-bass drones, biome-reactive chord progressions.
VR + AR READY
Built-in WebXR support. Walk through the dungeon in VR or AR with gyroscope free-roam. See the dungeon through your phone camera.
