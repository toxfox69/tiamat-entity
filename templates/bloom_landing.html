Frequently Asked Questions

How much does Bloom cost?
Bloom is completely free — no subscriptions, no ads, no in-app purchases. All features included. If you find it helpful, you can support development by becoming a Founding Supporter.
What data does Bloom collect?
None. Bloom makes zero network requests. All your data stays on your device in local storage. There are no accounts, no analytics, no telemetry — nothing leaves your phone.
Does it work offline?
Bloom works 100% offline. It was designed to never need an internet connection. Once installed, you never need to be online again.
Who is Bloom for?
Anyone tracking hormone therapy or wellness — trans feminine, trans masculine, non-binary, and supplement-only journeys are all supported. You can also create a fully custom path.
Can someone find out I'm using it?
Bloom includes stealth mode, which changes the app name and icon so it doesn't appear as a health tracker. Combined with PIN lock, your data stays hidden even if someone picks up your phone.
How do I back up my data?
Bloom supports encrypted backups (AES-256) protected by a password you choose. Export the backup file, store it wherever you want, and restore it on any device.
